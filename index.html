    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about SARF financing.

    How is SARF different from a traditional loan?
    SARF is not a loan - it's asset-backed commodity financing that is always interest-free. There are zero origination fees, no interest charges, and your total repayment amount is fixed and agreed on day one. You always know exactly what you owe.
    How does it work?
    SARF buys a commodity on your behalf and sells it to you at a fixed, agreed-upon price with deferred payment. You then choose to either keep the commodity or allow SARF to sell it on your behalf for cash. Either way, your total cost is transparent and locked in upfront - no interest, no surprises.
    Do I need a credit score to apply?
    No. SARF does not require a FICO score or run a hard credit pull. We analyze your bank-verified income, salary cycle, and cash-flow patterns to build a real-time picture of your ability to repay.
    How fast can I get approved?
    Most applicants receive a rate decision within 60 seconds. Once you connect your bank account, SARF instantly verifies your income and generates a personalized, fixed-rate offer.
    Are there any hidden fees?
    None. SARF charges $0 origination fees, $0 prepayment penalties, and $0 late-payment surcharges. The total cost of your financing is agreed upfront and never changes.
    How do repayments work?
    Repayments are automatically scheduled to align with your salary deposits - typically bi-weekly. This means payments are pulled when you have money, reducing missed-payment risk and making budgeting effortless.
    Can I lower my rate after applying?
    Yes. Adding a guarantor or providing collateral can reduce your rate by up to 3.5%. These options are completely optional and can be configured before you finalize your agreement.
    Is my financial data secure?
    Absolutely. SARF uses bank-level encryption and read-only access to verify your income. We never store your bank credentials, and your data is never shared with third parties.
